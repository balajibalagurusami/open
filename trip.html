7-Day Travel Itinerary

Day 1 - Delhi Sightseeing (April 26)

Indira Gandhi Memorial Museum, Rashtrapati Bhavan and Parliament House, India Gate, Humayun's Tomb, Red Fort. Return to Gurugram.

Day 2 - Agra Visit (April 27)

Visit Taj Mahal and Agra Fort. Return to Gurugram for overnight stay.

Day 3 - Delhi to Manali (April 28)

Full day journey with stops at Shawl Factory, Bilaspur Lake, Pandoh Dam, and Sundernagar Lake.

Day 4 - Manali Sightseeing (April 29)

Solang Valley, Nehru Kund, Him Valley, Atal Tunnel, and Sissu Waterfalls or Rohtang Pass.

Day 5 - Manali Local Tour (April 30)

Visit Hadimba Temple, Club House, Tibetan Monastery, Van Vihar, and Vashisht Temple.

Day 6 - Kullu Visit (May 1)

River rafting in Kullu, visit to Shawl Factory and shopping. Return to Manali for night stay.

Day 7 - Return to Gurugram (May 2)

Check out from hotel and return to Gurugram for night stay.
